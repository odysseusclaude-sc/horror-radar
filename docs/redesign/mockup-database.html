⚡ What is OPS (Overperformance Score)?

OPS measures how much a game is outperforming its peers in the first 90 days after release. A score of 60+ signals a potential breakout. It combines 5 real-time engagement signals:

📈
Velocity
35% weight
Review growth rate vs. age-adjusted median
🔄
Decay
20% weight
Week 2-4 velocity held vs. launch week
⭐
Reviews
15% weight
Total review count vs. price-adjusted median
▶
YouTube
15% weight
Creator coverage + view performance
🎮
CCU
15% weight
Peak concurrent players vs. peer median
