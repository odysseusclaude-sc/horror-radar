From [[Impressionism by Natalia Brodskaya]],
To judge from the remaining fragments, the canvas was rather dark. The static figures bring mannequins to mind. Monet had not yet got past the profusion of detail characteristic of genre painting. A young girl sets out the dishes on a tablecloth, and a dog is running in the foreground. Someone has carved initials and dates on the trunk of a birch tree, and behind it a young man is hiding. But already there is something here that points towards Monet’s future: the sun, as it pierces through the greenery of the trees, fragments it into small, juxtaposed patches, and the colored shadows on the women’s elegant dresses are painted with pure colors.
