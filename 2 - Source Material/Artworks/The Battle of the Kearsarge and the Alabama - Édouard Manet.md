it was the landscape in this painting, not the dynamics of the battle, that impressed all who saw it. The sea in Manet’s painting is alive. It is a uniform green without any reflections of colour; the impression of waves is solely created by some white touches. Monet was undoubtedly impressed by these seascapes as a French Norman.
