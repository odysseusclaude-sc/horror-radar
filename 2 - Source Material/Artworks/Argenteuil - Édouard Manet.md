From [[Impressionism by Natalia Brodskaya]],
The same characters, Rodolphe Leenhoff and his partner are seated on the riverbank in the midst of boats, which bob up and down around them. Their striped clothing creates such a shimmering effect of color on the canvas that this alone gives the painting an Impressionist appearance. Here, the landscape is rightfully part of the composition and becomes immediately impressionistic. The water is painted with broken brushwork and reflects the blue sky, the green trees, and the buildings of Argenteuil on the other bank.
