**Might not have a rice cooker.**

**Gyeranmari**
**Might not have blender**. Only need eggs, soy sauce, carrots and green onions. Lightly oil and then roll and keep making layers.

**Steamed Egg**
2 eggs, same volume of water, add green onions and carrots then put some salt. Put in a microwaveable container, microwave 5 minutes, put some sesame oil and done. **Might not have a microwaveable container**.

**Marinated Eggs**
Place eggs in water to boil. Add vinegar and salt and boil the eggs. 7 minutes later. Put them in ice. Sauce for marination is soy sauce, sugar, water, green onion, carrots, onion, sesame seed and sesame oil. Pour over the eggs, fully submerge them, use something to press the eggs down maybe. Set it aside overnight in the fridge. 

**Pickled Vegetables Sauce**
Equal parts soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and water. 
Soy pickled leek, green onion bundles, garlic (need a couple of weeks), shitake mushrooms. 

**Lotus Salad**
Black sesame, mayo, honey, vinegar, and blanche lotus root. Blanche lotus root is made by boiling lotus roots in water and add a little bit of vinegar so they dont turn brown. Then cold plunge them.

**Kimchi sauce**: Gochugaru, fish sauce, salted shrimp, minced garlic, and sugar. 

Leek kimchi, saute w fish sauce and mix it and rest 10 minutes. Put in the Kimchi sauce and mix well.
